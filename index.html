How to play

1. Use your voice to control the Pacman.

2. Say "START" or "BEGIN" to idicate that you want to start the game.

3. Use a phrase that contains any directional words to move the Pacman:

   (e.g. "Move left", "Go east", "Move up", "Go south")

4. You can also say "STOP" or "halt" to stop the Pacman from moving.

5. You may also restart the game by either saying "RESTART" or "RESET"

6. Say "PAUSE" to pause the game.

7. Say "CONTINUE" or "RESUME" to continue the game.

When you're ready, say "START" or idicate that you want to begin the game!
