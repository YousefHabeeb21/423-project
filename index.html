How to play

1. Use your voice to control the Pacman.

2. Say "START" to start the game.

3. Use a phrase that contains any directional words to move the Pacman:

(e.g. "Move left", "Go right", "Move up", "Go down")

4. You can also say "STOP" to stop the Pacman from moving.

5. Say "PAUSE" to pause the game.

6. Say "CONTINUE" to continue the game.

When you're ready, say "START" to begin the game!
